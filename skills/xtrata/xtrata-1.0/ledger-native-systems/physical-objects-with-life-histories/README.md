# Physical Objects with Life Histories

Physical items can be represented by lifecycle graphs covering manufacture, ownership, restoration, transport, exhibition, usage, and repair instead of only a static certificate of authenticity.

## Focus

- preserve provenance beyond ownership alone
- track cultural and maintenance history over time
- bind physical identity to a richer ledger narrative
