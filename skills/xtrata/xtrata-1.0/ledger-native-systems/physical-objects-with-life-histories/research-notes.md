# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- binding a physical item to its digital manifest without opening obvious spoofing paths
- modeling partial replacement while preserving meaningful authenticity signals
- balancing transparency with security and location privacy
- deciding which custodians or inspectors are trusted enough to author lifecycle events

## Open questions

- which object classes benefit most from a graph rather than a simple certificate?
- how should disputed provenance or restoration claims be represented?
- can maintenance incentives improve longevity without turning every repair into a speculative market?
