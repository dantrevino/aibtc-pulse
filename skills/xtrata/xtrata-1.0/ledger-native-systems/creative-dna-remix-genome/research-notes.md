# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- preventing dependency graphs from becoming too expensive to traverse
- defining transforms that are expressive enough across media types
- handling revocable licenses without breaking immutable provenance
- deciding how far automatic royalty recursion should extend in deep graphs

## Open questions

- should graph edges be weighted by contribution, runtime usage, or creator-declared policy?
- when does a remix become a new root work rather than a derivative branch?
- what index views must be reproducible directly from chain data?
