# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- handling large or private datasets while preserving trustworthy references
- standardizing claim granularity across disciplines
- preventing incentive systems from favoring trivial replications over important ones
- capturing execution environments precisely without freezing innovation

## Open questions

- what is the minimal schema for a useful claim graph?
- how should failed replications be represented without collapsing nuance?
- which research communities could adopt a lightweight version first?
