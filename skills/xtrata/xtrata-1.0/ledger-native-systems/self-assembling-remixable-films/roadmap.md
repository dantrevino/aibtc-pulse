# Roadmap

## Phase 0

- define scene, layer, and cut manifest schemas
- choose a short-form film example for the first graph

## Phase 1

- publish one film graph and at least one alternate cut
- build a cut comparison and lineage viewer

## Phase 2

- add scene-level licensing, soundtrack routing, and restoration records
- test dynamic assembly for localized or personalized cuts

## Phase 3

- expand into long-form productions and cross-studio collaborative workflows
