# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- managing large media payloads without losing the value of canonical graph objects
- expressing precise timeline edits in a compact, interoperable format
- handling layered rights across actors, score, footage, and derivative editions
- keeping playback deterministic enough for verification while remaining practical

## Open questions

- what is the smallest useful unit: scene, shot, frame range, or edit block?
- how should audience-generated cuts respect licensing and moral rights?
- which film workflows could adopt this without requiring full production replacement?
