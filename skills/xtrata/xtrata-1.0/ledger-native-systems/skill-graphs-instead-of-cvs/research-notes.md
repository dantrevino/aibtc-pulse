# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- preventing sybil attacks and reciprocal endorsement spam
- respecting privacy while still making skill evidence useful
- representing non-digital or offline work with enough trust
- avoiding extractive ranking systems that turn people into leaderboard objects

## Open questions

- which graph signals actually correlate with competence and reliability?
- how should disputed claims or failed projects be represented?
- what is the right balance between self-curation and third-party attestation?
