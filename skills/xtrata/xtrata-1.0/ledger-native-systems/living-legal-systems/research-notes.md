# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- distinguishing authoritative sources from commentary and machine summaries
- encoding legal ambiguity without pretending to eliminate it
- reconciling conflicting jurisdictions, translations, and publication practices
- avoiding false certainty when graph visualizations simplify contested law

## Open questions

- what clause granularity is practical for high-value legal graph traversal?
- how should overturned or superseded precedent remain visible?
- which legal domains are structured enough for early adoption?
