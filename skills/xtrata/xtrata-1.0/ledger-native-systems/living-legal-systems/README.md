# Living Legal Systems

Legal frameworks can be expressed as linked graphs of statutes, precedents, amendments, interpretations, and jurisdictional variants rather than disconnected documents and databases.

## Focus

- make legal lineage explicit
- preserve how rules evolve through cases and amendments
- support machine-readable inspection of precedent and change
