# Research Notes

## Key research challenges

- building ontologies broad enough for many domains without collapsing nuance
- scaling graph traversal and search across huge heterogeneous datasets
- governing moderation, cultural sensitivity, and contested provenance
- preventing one index provider or ontology from becoming the hidden gatekeeper

## Open questions

- what minimal base schema can unify these domains while staying useful?
- how should local community archives federate into a broader cultural graph?
- what stewardship and funding models keep the graph public over long time horizons?
