# Roadmap

## Phase 0

- define a base artifact and relationship model
- choose a small but intentionally cross-domain seed corpus

## Phase 1

- publish a federated prototype explorer with typed cross-domain links
- test curation and ontology-mapping workflows

## Phase 2

- add collection governance, grant funding, and stewardship models
- explore interoperability with domain-specific subgraphs

## Phase 3

- expand toward public cultural infrastructure with many participating archives and communities
