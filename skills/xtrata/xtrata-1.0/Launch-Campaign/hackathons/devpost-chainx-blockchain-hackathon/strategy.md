# Devpost: ChainX The Blockchain Hackathon Strategy

## Event snapshot
- Listing window observed: 2026-02-11 to 2026-02-19 (online).
- Public listing indicates small participation at verification time.
- Prize details were not clearly exposed in accessible public pages.
- Confidence: medium-low (listing-derived).

## Recommended entry
Build **Xtrata Proof-of-Reconstruction Mini Verifier**.

## Why this is the best fit
- Very short duration favors a focused technical prototype.
- A compact verifier demo can be shipped quickly and judged easily.
- Demonstrates infrastructure depth without requiring full product breadth.

## Product concept
Input a token/content reference and prove whether media can be reconstructed
deterministically from on-chain chunk state and dependency graph.

## 60-second pitch
Most on-chain media projects stop at pointer metadata. Xtrata Verifier checks
whether an asset is truly reconstructable from chain data and returns a proof
summary with hash integrity and dependency status. It gives builders and
collectors an immediate trust signal instead of blind metadata assumptions.

## Hackathon scope
Must-have:
- Single-page verifier UI.
- Hash + chunk reconstruction check.
- Pass/fail proof output with readable diagnostics.

Stretch:
- Exportable proof report.
- Batch verification endpoint.

## Submission checklist
- Keep scope strict to one user flow.
- Include before/after comparison with pointer-only metadata.
- Confirm final prize details directly on event page at submission time.
