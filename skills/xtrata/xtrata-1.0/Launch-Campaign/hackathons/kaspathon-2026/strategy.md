# Kaspathon 2026 Strategy

## Event snapshot
- Kaspathon was observed as an active campaign with KAS-denominated prizes and
  explicit requirement for meaningful Kaspa integration.
- Published terms indicate substantial reward pools distributed in KAS.
- Confidence: medium-high (official pages and terms available; timeline details
  should be rechecked on submission portal).

## Recommended entry
Build **Audionals KAS Stream** (real-time creator payment rail).

## Why this is the best fit
- Kaspa performance narrative aligns with micropayment-heavy creator use cases.
- Audionals naturally demonstrates high-frequency usage and payout flows.
- Meets requirement for meaningful protocol integration more clearly than a
  generic cross-chain demo.

## Product concept
Stream tiny creator payments in KAS per usage event:
- Session-based usage metering.
- Real-time split payouts.
- On-chain receipt trail for creator revenue transparency.

## 60-second pitch
Audionals KAS Stream converts media usage into real-time creator revenue.
Instead of monthly opaque statements, creators receive transparent, event-linked
KAS payouts as usage happens. The system demonstrates why high-throughput chains
unlock new business models for digital media rights.

## Hackathon scope
Must-have:
- Wallet flow and KAS payment transaction loop.
- Usage meter to payout conversion logic.
- Creator payout dashboard with transparent receipts.

Stretch:
- Dynamic pricing presets by media type and usage rights.
- Team/collaborator split routing.

## Submission checklist
- Verify final submission deadline in the event portal before build lock.
- Keep all integrations open-source and reproducible.
- Include a failure-mode demo (network interruption and retry behavior).
