# Devpost: HackFW 2026 Strategy

## Event snapshot
- Listing window observed: 2026-04-03 to 2026-05-02.
- Event framing emphasizes convergent technology and practical impact.
- Small direct prize pool in listing, with value also in partnerships/exposure.
- Confidence: medium-high.

## Recommended entry
Build **Xtrata Evidence Vault for Regulated Workflows**.

## Why this is the best fit
- HackFW judging language highlights technical readiness and real-world
  applicability.
- Xtrata can be positioned as infrastructure for compliance, provenance, and
  immutable records in manufacturing/health/defense-adjacent workflows.
- This narrative is stronger for regional industry audiences than pure NFT
  storytelling.

## Product concept
Teams upload critical process artifacts (images, docs, logs) and generate a
deterministic on-chain evidence record with traceable dependency links.

## 60-second pitch
Xtrata Evidence Vault gives regulated teams a tamper-resistant source of truth
for process evidence. Every artifact is hashed, sealed, and reconstructable,
creating a durable audit trail for operations where missing provenance leads to
cost, delay, or compliance risk.

## Hackathon scope
Must-have:
- Evidence ingest flow.
- On-chain record creation.
- Viewer that reconstructs evidence chain and timestamps.

Stretch:
- Alerting when evidence dependencies are missing.
- Export-ready compliance report format.

## Submission checklist
- Open with a concrete industry pain point.
- Show one full audit trace from upload to verification.
- Align presentation with HackFW judging dimensions.
