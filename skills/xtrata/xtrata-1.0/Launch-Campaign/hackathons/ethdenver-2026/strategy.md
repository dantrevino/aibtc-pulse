## Recommended entry
Build **Xtrata Recursive Media ProofKit** with one sponsor-specific bounty angle.

## Why this is the best fit
- ETHDenver rewards strong demos plus sponsor-specific alignment.
- Xtrata can position as core infra for verifiable media/data provenance.
- A modular ProofKit demo can be retargeted to multiple bounty tracks.

## Product concept
A toolkit and demo app that:
- Seals media/data manifests.
- Resolves recursive dependencies.
- Exports proof packets for marketplaces, wallets, and creator platforms.

## 60-second pitch
Xtrata ProofKit turns on-chain media from opaque blobs into verifiable modular
graphs. Builders can prove provenance, dependency integrity, and reconstructable
state in one flow, enabling better trust and interoperability across creator
and marketplace products.

## Hackathon scope
Must-have:
- End-to-end proof generation and verification flow.
- One polished demo use case (creator release or marketplace ingestion).
- Sponsor track mapping slide with exact bounty relevance.

Stretch:
- Multi-chain adapter demo.
- AI-assisted provenance explainer in UI.

## Submission checklist

- Include technical architecture and real-world adoption path.
