# Devpost: The Midwest Blockathon 2026 Strategy

## Event snapshot
- Listing window observed: 2026-03-06 to 2026-03-08.
- Listing indicates in-person, United States, with a small cash prize pool.
- Confidence: medium-low (listing-derived, direct event page not consistently
  accessible during verification).

## Recommended entry
Build **Audionals Creator Capsule (Campus Edition)**.

## Why this is the best fit
- Short in-person events reward demos that are easy to understand quickly.
- Creator-facing narrative performs better than deep protocol explanations in
  broad hackathon judging rooms.
- Audionals delivers fast visual/aural impact with clear user outcomes.

## Product concept
Creators upload a short clip, set usage terms, and publish a shareable on-chain
license capsule. Collectors or collaborators can preview rights, usage scope,
and proof of origin instantly.

## 60-second pitch
Audionals Creator Capsule gives student and indie creators a way to publish
music rights that are machine-readable from day one. Instead of uploading files
and hoping for attribution, they mint a verifiable rights capsule that can be
licensed, remixed, and tracked transparently.

## Hackathon scope
Must-have:
- Upload + mint flow.
- Rights summary card (creator, allowed use, attribution terms).
- Shareable URL for judges to test quickly.

Stretch:
- Remix permission toggle and collaborator split demo.

## Submission checklist
- Keep onboarding under 90 seconds.
- Pre-record fallback demo in case venue internet is unstable.
- Verify final rules and prize amounts directly in event dashboard.
