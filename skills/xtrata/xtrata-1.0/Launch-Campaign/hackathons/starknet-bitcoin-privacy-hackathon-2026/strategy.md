## Why this is the best fit
- Strong alignment with Bitcoin + privacy narrative.
- Combines Xtrata’s public permanence/provenance with privacy-preserving rights

## Product concept
- Public layer: content fingerprints and provenance anchors (Xtrata model).
- Private layer: usage terms, buyer identity privacy, and negotiated licensing
  logic in Starknet-compatible privacy flows.
- Settlement outputs: verifiable receipts without exposing sensitive terms.

## 60-second pitch
Xtrata Private Licensing Relay gives creators public proof of origin and private
control over commercial licensing terms. Teams can verify authenticity and
settlement status without revealing confidential licensing data, combining
Bitcoin-aligned permanence with privacy-preserving commerce.

## Hackathon scope

- Demo with one creator and one buyer scenario.

Stretch:

- Policy templates for reusable private licensing patterns.
