# Platform Playbooks

## X / Twitter
- Frequency: 1-2 posts/day, 2 threads/week
- Content mix:
  - 40% product insights
  - 30% demos and clips
  - 20% creator showcases
  - 10% community wins
- Best formats: short clips, 2-4 image carousels, threads
- CTA: viewer link, mint flow, developer guide

## YouTube
- Frequency: 1 long-form video/week, 2-3 Shorts/week
- Long-form topics:
  - How Xtrata inscriptions are reconstructed
  - Recursion explained with real examples
- Shorts topics:
  - 15-30s viewer demos
  - visual before/after (on-chain vs off-chain)

## TikTok / Reels
- Frequency: 3-4 clips/week
- Style: fast visual hooks, minimal text, clear captions
- Hook examples:
  - "This app is on-chain. Not IPFS."
  - "On-chain audio engine in 15 seconds."

## Discord / Farcaster / Telegram
- Weekly: progress update + drop teasers
- Monthly: AMA or demo
- Encourage community remix or recursive challenges

## Reddit
- Monthly long-form post:
  - "What is Xtrata?"
  - "How recursion changes NFTs"
- AMA in r/Stacks or r/CryptoArt

## Medium / Mirror
- Monthly deep-dive:
  - "The memory layer for Bitcoin"
  - "How recursion enables modular media"
