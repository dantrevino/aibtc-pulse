# Xtrata Cross-Platform Content and Response Handbook

Use this as the execution-ready operating doc for launch campaign posting and
community response management.

## 1) Campaign Operating Context

## Positioning
Xtrata is the on-chain memory layer for Bitcoin: a permanent, recursive data
platform on Stacks that powers modular media and applications.

## Primary CTAs
- Explore the viewer: https://xtrata.xyz
- Mint your first inscription: https://xtrata.xyz
- Read the developer guide: https://xtrata.xyz

## Core audience segments
1. Builders and developers
2. Artists, musicians, and creators
3. Collectors, curators, and galleries
4. Marketplaces and platform partners
5. General crypto audience

## Messaging pillars
1. Permanence: large on-chain data with deterministic reconstruction
2. Recursion: inscriptions reference inscriptions for modular apps and media
3. Compatibility: SIP-009 compatibility with wallets and marketplaces
4. Scale: chunked uploads and batch reads for efficiency
5. Use cases: apps, sound engines, datasets, recursive media

## Guardrails
- Use only official Xtrata accounts and approved team identities.
- Keep claims factual and product-verifiable.
- Do not auto-reply without human approval.
- Respect platform rules, rate limits, and subreddit/server-specific rules.
