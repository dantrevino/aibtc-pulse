Before You Inscribe

Use Xverse and check fees manually

Avoid Leather wallet for this flow. Use Xverse for a better inscription experience.

  • Always inspect the fee the wallet suggests before sending.
  • Manually increase or decrease the fee to the right amount for current conditions.
  • If the wallet returns errors, the most likely cause is the fee being too low.
