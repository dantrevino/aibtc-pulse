# Astro Blaster Expansion Roadmap

## Vision
Evolve Astro Blaster from a short arcade loop into a deeper session game with stronger story context, longer progression arcs, and richer build diversity.

## Delivery Status Snapshot
- Stage 1: baseline complete (pacing runtime, narrative runtime, shield baseline, EMP baseline, threat + reputation support).
- Current focus: Stage 2 entry work on hazard depth, upgrade identity, and clarity systems.

## Target Outcomes
- Increase average successful run time without padding.
- Make upgrades less frequent but more meaningful.
- Add strategic layer depth (shields, specials, risk/reward choices).
- Improve narrative continuity across waves/sectors.

## Stage 1 - Narrative Spine + Session Pacing
### Goals
- Introduce a story arc that unfolds over sectors.
- Slow early upgrade cadence so progression is earned.

### Planned Features
- Sector briefing cards with short story beats.
- Mid-sector comms/transmissions (5-10 sec) between major waves.
- “Threat escalation” messaging tied to gameplay changes.
- Pre-wave threat card showing expected enemy composition for the next wave.
- New pacing targets:
  - First major upgrade later than current baseline.
  - Longer tension windows between big power spikes.

### Exit Criteria
- Story beats are understandable without blocking flow.
- No sharp early-game overpowering.

## Stage 2 - Upgrade Depth and Build Identity
### Goals
- Expand upgrade tiers, trade-offs, and weapon identities.

### Planned Features
- Tiered upgrades (Mk I-IV) with branching paths.
- More projectile behavior families:
  - Precision, spread, piercing, chaining, tracking, suppression.
- Faction reputation narrative track that changes transmissions by player behavior.
- Negative/curse boxes (red herrings) with clear telegraphing.
- Upgrade economy rebalance:
  - Fewer offers, stronger impact per offer.
  - Context-aware offers by current build and wave pressure.

### Exit Criteria
- At least 4 viable build archetypes.
- No single dominant “always pick” line.

## Stage 3 - Shields, Special Weapons, and Tactical Systems
### Goals
- Add survivability and clutch systems that increase decision depth.

### Planned Features
- Shield families:
  - Front arc shield.
  - Rotating shield drones.
  - Emergency reactive shield.
- Special weapons with cooldown/resource cost:
  - EMP pulse.
  - Rail burst.
  - Drone swarm strike.
  - Gravity well trap.
- Counterplay: enemies and hazards that punish autopilot.

### Exit Criteria
- Specials feel powerful but non-spammable.
- Shield systems support skilled play, not invulnerability loops.

## Stage 4 - Mode Expansion
### Goals
- Add replayable modes with clear identity.

### Candidate Modes
- Story Run (long-form progression).
- Survival Ramp (infinite pressure scaling).
- Weekly Challenge (fixed seed/modifier set).
- Hardcore (limited lives, score multipliers).
- Daily mission modifiers (optional objective set with constrained score multipliers).

### Exit Criteria
- Modes have unique strategy differences, not cosmetic variants.
- Leaderboard/scoring rules remain clear and testable per mode.

## Stage 5 - Balance, Polish, and Retention Hardening
### Goals
- Tune difficulty curve, readability, and progression fairness.

### Planned Features
- Full tuning pass (spawn curves, upgrade cadence, boss pressure).
- UX pass for clarity (telegraphs, damage feedback, pickups).
- Content cadence pass (new enemy behavior combinations per sector).

### Exit Criteria
- Engagement increases with lower frustration spikes.
- Performance, score integrity, and restart stability remain solid.
